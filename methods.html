How We Built the Dataset

Understanding the data behind the visuals

Data Collection

To analyze the mock neck trend, we collected TikTok data from two categories: general mock neck content and PARKE-specific content. Using a data scraping tool, we gathered information including views, likes, comments, and captions from multiple videos across each category.

What We Measured

From this dataset, we focused on key engagement metrics such as total views, likes, and comments, along with engagement rates (likes and comments relative to views). We also analyzed caption language to identify common themes and repeated phrases that appear across high-performing posts.

Why This Matters

This approach allows us to move beyond just observing trends visually and instead understand how engagement contributes to their growth. By comparing two datasets, we can see how different types of content perform and how audience interaction helps reinforce a trend over time.
